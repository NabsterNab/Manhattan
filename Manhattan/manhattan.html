Who Are We?

Café Manhattan, located at the entrance to the city of Tetouan, and more precisely in front of the emphatic monument that symbolizes the white dove for which Tetouan is known.

What Do We Offer

Café Manhattan is known for its breakfasts and also quick dishes it serves. ideal for going with family or friends. Café Manhattan is also known for its bakery where you will find a variety of breads, sweet cakes and all kinds of tradional sweets
